I'm a Computer Science undergrad at Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology, based in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh. Alongside code, I run design for two student communities — Graphic Head & Joint Secretary at CSWD and Graphic Team Lead at Tech4Hack — so I end up thinking about interfaces from both sides of the screen.

I like the parts of engineering most people skip: naming things well, writing the boring test, leaving things better than I found them. Outside of that, I'm usually deep in a hackathon, teaching someone something, playing guitar, or planning the next trip.
